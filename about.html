Our Story

A Family Recipe That Started It All

In 1987, Antonio Rossi brought his family's secret pizza recipe from Chicago to Texas. What started as a small pizzeria with just 8 tables has grown into a beloved local chain serving thousands of customers every week.

Our commitment has never wavered: use only the freshest ingredients, hand-stretch every dough ball, and bake each pizza to perfection in our signature wood-fired ovens. We're not just making pizza—we're crafting experiences that bring families and friends together.

Today, we're proud to operate four locations across the Houston area, each maintaining the same dedication to quality and authenticity that Antonio established over three decades ago.

Antonio Rossi Founder & Master Pizza Chef
Our Journey

35 Years of Pizza Excellence

1987

The Beginning

Antonio Rossi opens the first Chicago Pizza in The Woodlands with just 8 tables and a wood-fired oven.

1995

First Expansion

Opened our second location in Spring, Texas, bringing authentic pizza to more communities.

2003

Award Recognition

Named "Best Pizza in Houston" by Houston Chronicle readers for the first time.

2010

Catering Launched

Started our catering service to bring Chicago Pizza to events across Texas.

2018

Going Digital

Launched online ordering and delivery service, making it easier than ever to enjoy our pizza.

2024

Today

Four thriving locations, 500,000+ pizzas served, and still committed to Antonio's original vision.
